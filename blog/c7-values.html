Cultural Blog #7: Diamond in the rough

07/13/15

This week we were given a table of words and asked to pick which ones coincided with our happiest, proudest, etc moments. I looked at this list and only one of them really stood out to me. It was the word Challenge.

You may wonder why that word. Well honestly it's because any noteworthy thing that I have achieved it was because someone challenged me.I don't mean in the sense that I was challenged to a race or anything. I mean it in terms of being doubted. I was challenged through a good portion of my life to feel like I needed to prove myself. There were people in my life that made me feel like I couldn't be good enough. I would always challenge that by doing what they said I couldn't do. I always take the challenges to prove my worth because I know I'm worthy of everything I achieve.

I don't really know the answer to the next part. I can't remember the last time someone asked me for advice. I'm usually the one that listens to people when they need to talk. I don't know that I've offered any key advice lately though.

I don't know that the value I picked from this would help with stereotype threat but I do listen to people. I like to know all sides of the story and be something of a mediator. As far as cheering myself up at DBC usually some fresh air or coffee will do the trick for me.
